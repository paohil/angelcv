Project Overview

This project was part of Cebu Pacific’s destination content series, showcasing multiple destinations across the Philippines. I oversaw production across Cebu, Iloilo, Boracay, and Palawan, managing lean on-ground crews, coordinating talent, logistics, and itineraries while adapting to varying weather and location constraints. In many cases, I handled pre- and post-production for some locations while actively producing on-site for others. Content from these campaigns has also been featured during Cebu Pacific events, including large-scale displays such as the Burj Khalifa. This series continued into 2025, covering even more destinations across the Philippines.

Key Highlights

  • On-ground producer for one leg of a multi-destination travel campaign.
  • Managed lean crews, logistics, and location-based adjustments on shoot.
  • Balanced on-site production while overseeing pre and post-production for other destinations.
  • Campaign content featured in high-profile international brand activations.
