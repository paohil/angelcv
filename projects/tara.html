Short Film

Tara? Takbo!

Two track-and-field athletes aspire to make their own names in the sports industry until their social status hindered them to do so.

  • Project Type: Directing Class Final Project
  • Prod: Mi Casa Pelikula
  • Genre: Drama / Comedy
  • Date: 2023
  • Duration: 2-day shoot
  • Role(s): Producer, Line Producer, Production Manager, Assistant Director
  • Awards: The Filipino Festival 2023; Best Film & Best Actor (8th Sorok Short Film Festival PH); Asia-Pacific Youth Micro Movie Festival
View BTS

Project Overview

Tara? Takbo! is a narrative short film created as the final project for a directing class. The story follows two aspiring track-and-field athletes whose dreams are challenged by the realities of social inequality. As part of a small five-person main team, I took on multiple major roles, serving as Producer, Production Manager, Assistant Director, and Line Producer. I oversaw all the logistics, pre-production planning, daily shoot coordination, and overall logistics for our two-day production, ensuring the set ran smoothly while supporting both creative and technical needs.

Key Highlights

  • Took on four major roles (Producer, PM, AD, Line Producer) due to a lean five-person team.
  • Managed end-to-end production from pre-prod to shoot execution.
  • Coordinated a two-day out of town location shoot with limited time and resources.
  • Ensured smooth logistics, scheduling, and talent/crew management.
  • Supported the director in translating the concept into efficient shoot plans.
  • Successfully delivered a polished short film for finals despite a small team setup.
