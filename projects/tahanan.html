Short Film

Tahanan

Moving day turns into a verbal battleground when a rebellious daughter's refusal forces her mother to finally confront their painful past.

  • Project Type: Production Class Project
  • Team: 2-Man Team
  • Genre: Short Film / Drama
  • Date: 2022
  • Duration: 1-day shoot
  • Role(s): Director, Writer, Producer
  • Awards: 2022 MUD Studios 3 Shots Film Fest – Finalist (Top 20); Best Cinematography Nominee

Project Overview

Created for a production class that required films to be shot in only three continuous shots, Tahanan was executed by a two-person core team. I handled directing, writing, and production while working closely with my cinematographer-editor partner to craft a visually driven narrative. The film was recognized by MUD Studios’ 3 Shots Film Fest for its cinematography and storytelling.

Key Highlights

  • Directed a three-shot film requiring precise blocking and visual planning.
  • Handled writing, directing, and production simultaneously.
  • Executed a full narrative with a two-person technical team.
  • Earned finalist placement and a cinematography nomination at MUD Studios.
