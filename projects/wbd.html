MAX Philippines Launch

Supervising multi-unit video production for the Philippine launch of MAX, featuring James and Oliver Phelps.

  • Project Type: Entertainment / Event Launch
  • Prod: MAX
  • Genre: Entertainment / Event Launch
  • Date: 2024
  • Duration: 1-day shoot (2 major setups)
  • Role(s): Video Team Production Coordinator
Wizards of Baking Event Launch

Project Overview

This project involved two major shoots in one day: a morning YouTube promo shoot for the Wizards of Baking campaign featuring James and Oliver Phelps, followed by full event coverage for the MAX Philippines launch in BGC. I led the coordination between the video production team and on-ground event operations, planning unit movements, assigning coverage areas, and managing equipment rentals and logistics across both shoots. I also worked closely with the on-ground team to align permit requirements and ensure smooth crew access. The role required strong logistical planning, fast decision-making, and clear communication to deliver complete coverage across platforms.

Key Highlights

  • Coordinated two major shoots in one day: YouTube promo + live launch event.
  • Planned multi-unit coverage, crew movements, and equipment logistics.
  • Served as the main bridge between video team and on-ground operations.
  • Ensured seamless coverage across event, social, and digital platforms.
