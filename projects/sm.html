SM Woman Fashion Campaign

Coordinating video production for a fashion-forward officewear campaign under SM Retail, among their other fashion campaigns.

Project Overview

This campaign was initially handled during my internship with Vibetribe Productions and produced for SM Woman with Sisi Advertising as the agency. After my internship, I continued working full-time with Vibetribe Productions, where I handled additional SM Fashion campaigns across multiple categories. I served as the main production coordinator for the video team, acting as the bridge between the agency, client, and crew. My responsibilities included managing client checks, coordinating call sheets, handling on-set logistics and crew needs, and ensuring clear communication throughout production. Beyond this campaign, I worked on various SM Fashion projects including kidswear, shoes, jewelry, and accessories.

Key Highlights

  • Coordinated video production for a retail fashion advertising campaign.
  • Acted as the primary liaison between agency, client, and video team.
  • Managed on-set logistics, call sheets, and client checks.
  • Delivered a 60s main video with multiple edit-downs for campaign use.
