MG Automotive Campaign

Producing a large-scale multi-day automotive shoot featuring multiple vehicle hero videos.

  • Client: MG Philippines
  • Category: Automotive Advertising
  • Date: 2025
  • Duration: 3-day out-of-town shoot
  • Role: Video Team Producer
  • Deliverables: 1 main hero video, 5 individual car videos
View Campaign

Project Overview

This was a large-scale, out-of-town automotive production involving multiple units assigned to different vehicles and the use of a Hydra Arm rig. I produced the video team side of the project, overseeing pre-production through production within a one-month timeline. The shoot covered five vehicles, each with its own dedicated video, alongside a main hero film, requiring tight coordination across units to maintain efficiency across three shoot days. This is one of several automotive campaigns I’ve handled, including projects for Toyota and Mercedes.

Key Highlights

  • Led video team production from pre-production to a 3-day multi-unit out-of-town shoot within a one-month timeline
  • Mounted multiple units, including specialized Hydra Arm rigging
  • Delivered 5 individual car videos plus a main hero film
  • Led video crew coordination, logistics, and agency alignment across production
