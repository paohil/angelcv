Short Film

Sa mga Mata ng Bata

Struggling with his class presentation about his father, an elementary student named Philip turns to his infamous politician father for help, leading to an unexpected outburst inside the classroom.

  • Project Type: Thesis Film (Upper Year)
  • Prod: Anonymous Jeepney Productions
  • Genre: Drama
  • Date: 2022
  • Duration: 3-day shoot (Out of Town)
  • Role(s): Associate Producer, Production Manager
  • Awards: Realiflm Adamson – Silver Award (2023); Gawad Alternatibo – 3rd Place Short Feature (2023); Official Selection – Diwa Filipino Film Showcase of Seattle (2023); Pelikultura (2023)
Watch Film

Project Overview

I joined this upper-year thesis film as a freshman intern, taking on major logistical roles for a small crew originally composed of eight members. Due to sudden illnesses within the team, we continued as a six-person crew, requiring me to juggle multiple responsibilities including logistics, scheduling, and crew coordination. The project involved working closely with a child actor and managing a full out-of-town production. The film later earned multiple recognitions in national and international festivals.

Key Highlights

  • Handled logistics for a small and shifting crew during an out-of-town production.
  • Worked closely with a child actor and managed tight shoot schedules.
  • Juggled multiple roles due to limited manpower.
  • Part of an award-winning thesis film with notable festival recognitions.
