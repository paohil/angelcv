Short Film

Guys! Action!

The high school multimedia club students aim to win Best Short Film in Cinemalaya Film Festival in order to avoid dissolution ordered by the school admin.

  • Project Type: Thesis Film
  • Prod: Kuhang Kuting Productions
  • Genre: Comedy / School Drama
  • Date: 2023
  • Duration: 3-day shoot (Out of Town)
  • Role(s): Assistant Director
  • Awards: Official Selection – San Diego Filipino Film Festival (Audience Award) (2024); Sinepiyu XVI – Interschool Narrative Category Finalist (2024)
Watch Film

Project Overview

This thesis film marked my first experience as Assistant Director in a large crew composed of older students who had already worked together before. I was responsible for mounting and directing a team managing six cast members across a three-day out-of-town shoot. Since the film was a “film in a film”, I also had to track and plan for the different aspect ratios, cinematography styles, and internal story elements required for each layer. The project challenged my leadership, communication, and organizational skills, ultimately earning festival selections and awards.

Key Highlights

  • First major AD role with a larger and more senior crew.
  • Managed six cast members and coordinated scenes across multiple locations.
  • Successfully mounted a three-day out-of-town production.
  • Film later selected by local and international festivals.
